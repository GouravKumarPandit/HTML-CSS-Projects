Mission

With more than 8000 students on roll and 59 faculties in different streams, Jamshedpur Co-operative College is committed to imparting higher education to the students of the steel city as well as the tribal students from the adjoining rural areas. The college is sensitive to the needs of the students coming from various cross-sections of social strata and income groups.
